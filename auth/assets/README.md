## Required Images

### Background Image
Please save the "Thinker with Starry Night" image as:
- **Filename**: `thinker-starry-night.jpg`
- **Path**: `auth/assets/thinker-starry-night.jpg`
- **Description**: The decorative background image showing "The Thinker" silhouette against Van Gogh's Starry Night background

This image will be displayed on the right side of the login page.

## Image Requirements
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or similar portrait orientation
